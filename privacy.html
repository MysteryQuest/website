What We Collect

Analytics Data (Anonymous)

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our site:

  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Geographic location (country/city level only)
  • Device type and browser information
  • Referral sources (where you came from)

Note: This data is anonymized and aggregated. We cannot identify individual users.

Voting Data

When you vote on "Real or Hoax?" investigations:

  • Your vote is stored in our database
  • A cookie prevents duplicate voting from the same browser
  • No personally identifiable information is collected

Email Subscriptions (Optional)

If you choose to subscribe to updates:

  • Email address is stored securely
  • You can unsubscribe at any time
  • We will never share or sell your email

What We DON'T Collect

  • We do NOT require accounts or logins
  • We do NOT collect names, addresses, or phone numbers
  • We do NOT use tracking pixels in emails
  • We do NOT sell or share your data with third parties
  • We do NOT use invasive tracking or fingerprinting

How We Use Your Data

Analytics & Improvement

We analyze traffic patterns to improve content and user experience. For example, if many visitors leave on a specific page, we know to improve that content.

Community Features

Voting data is aggregated to show community consensus on investigations. Individual votes are never publicly displayed.

Communication

If you subscribe to updates, we'll email you about new D-LOG reports and significant site updates. No spam, no marketing emails.

Cookies

We use cookies for essential functionality:

Essential Cookies

Required for voting functionality and preventing duplicate votes. These cannot be disabled.

Analytics Cookies (Google Analytics)

Help us understand site usage. You can opt out using browser settings or Google's opt-out tool.
