La Mojana, Colombia

La Mojana was a small town in Colombia where the mysterious disappearance of three American tourists in 1976 remains one of the most enduring unsolved mysteries in history. The incident, which occurred on July 30, 1976, involved the disappearance of three U.S. citizens while on a fishing trip in the Gulf of California.
